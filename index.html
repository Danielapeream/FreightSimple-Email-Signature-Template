How to add your signature in Outlook:
1. Open the generator and fill in your details
2. Click Copy HTML
3. Go to outlook.com → Settings ⚙️ → View all settings → Mail → Compose and reply
4. Click into the signature box and paste with Ctrl+V
5. Save — done!

How to add in Gmail:
1. Open Gmail → Settings → See all settings → Signature
2. Click into the signature box and paste with Ctrl+V
3. Save
